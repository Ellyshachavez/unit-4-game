Crystal Collector



You will be given a random number at the start of the game.


There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specfic amount of points to your total score.


You win the game by matching your total score to a randomly generated number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.


The value of the crystal is hidden from you until you click the crystal.


Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
